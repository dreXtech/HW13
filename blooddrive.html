Every summer, Lake Michigan Community College challenges one of its rival schools to a
contest to see who can reach the goal of 2,000 donors. The challenge takes place during
the Michigan State Fair in Lansing, MI. This year, our challenger is Berrien Springs College.
  • A single donation can help save three lives in as little as 48 hours
  • Every two seconds someone in the U.S. is in need of blood
  • Blood supplies are often critically short, so your help is needed
  • Please help us reach our goal of 2,000 donors
