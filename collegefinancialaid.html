Financial Aid

Financial Aid Each year, many LMCC students receive financial aid in one form or another. As participants in the U. S. Department of Education Quality Assurance Program, our mission is to assist students in finding financial resources to aid in obtaining their highest educational goals.

Types of financial aid available:

  • Student loans

  • Scholarships

  • Grants

  • Fee remissions

To be considered, you must file a Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA)/Renewal application. To be given priority, you must file by the deadline. (See the FAFSA application for dates.) All assistance awarded depends on the extent of a student's need and the amount of funds available.
