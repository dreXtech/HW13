Lake Michigan Community College Mission Statement
Lake Michigan Community College (LMCC) exists to provide the highest quality education to all individuals. To meet this end, LMCC will distinguish itself through:
  • Student Centered Agendas
  • Community Needs
  • Outstanding Teacher Performance
  • Ethical Behavior
  • Fairness
Lake Michigan Community College will always be there for its students through learning, caring, and providing the best possible education and learning environment for all who walk through its doors.
