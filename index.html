Welcome to Lake Michigan Community College (LMCC) Student Services. Our purpose is to help each student achieve a positive learning experience and participate in meaningful social activities. We desire that students feel comfortable with their surroundings, transition into the college setting easily, and have a place where they can seek assistance or counseling. For students away from home, family, and friends for the first time, college life can be both frightening and fascinating. Let Student Services help you connect with the community and campus culture. We offer a variety of services. Our student assistants and full-time staff are available from 7:30 a.m. to 7:30 p.m. Monday through Friday. Stop by to get acquainted, and feel free to call on us for information or any question you may have.

Lake Michigan Community College is located in southwestern Michigan along the shores of Lake Michigan. LMCC was founded over 80 years ago as a teaching institution as part of Michigan's commitment to higher education for all its citizens. Today, LMCC's goal is to be on the forefront of educational technology, to provide students with the kind of low cost, high quality education every one should have access to in the state of Michigan..

Our mission is to provide the highest quality public education at affordable prices. LMCC is committed to bringing education to the door steps to the greater community at large, and the entire state of Michigan through its commitment to cutting edge technology. Our online courses are rated in the top-ten of the Michigan Educational Review.
